Welcome to the FediGree concept!¶

Think of you are doing some genealogic research for your family, put things together in a software tool and at some day there is a mail in your inbox from another person doing research itself having an overlapping ancestor tree with you. From now on you share all data concerning the people in the overlapping tree having a full copy of all data in your own database.

This would be a valid case if you were using FediGree.

These pages are a starting point to find and discuss a concept to bring this to live. They are organised following the [arc42 template](https://arc42.org/).
